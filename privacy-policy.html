Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SivaDesigns collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you visit this website, contact me, or inquire about graphic design services, including brand identity, logo design, social media creatives, and promotional video content.

2. Information I Collect

I may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you contact me, request a quote, start a project, or communicate by email. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business or brand name
  • Your project details, goals, and design requirements
  • Any files, links, references, or brand materials you choose to share

3. How I Use Your Information

The information you provide may be used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Discuss your design project
  • Prepare proposals, quotes, invoices, or project timelines
  • Provide graphic design, branding, social media, or promotional video services
  • Communicate project updates and deliverables
  • Improve the website, services, and client experience

4. Website Analytics and Cookies

This website may use basic analytics tools, cookies, or similar technologies to understand how visitors use the website and to improve performance. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, although some website features may not function as intended.

5. Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services such as hosting providers, analytics tools, email services, embedded media, or external links. These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites or services linked from this website.

6. Sharing of Information

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your information may only be shared when required to complete a project, comply with legal obligations, process business operations, or protect rights, safety, and security.

7. Project Materials and Portfolio Use

Project materials, final designs, brand assets, testimonials, or case study details may be used in my portfolio, website, or promotional materials only where permitted by the project agreement or with appropriate consent. Confidential materials shared for a project will be handled with care.

8. Data Security

I take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Data Retention

I keep personal information only for as long as necessary to respond to inquiries, provide services, maintain business records, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, or support future project communication.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Ask questions about how your information is used

11. Children’s Privacy

This website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact me so it can be removed.

12. Updates to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
